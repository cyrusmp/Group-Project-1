Facts About Climate Change

Rising Temperatures

According to a report conducted by Nasa, the planet's average surface temperatures has risen about 2 degrees Fahrenheit, with most of the warming occuring in the past 40 years.

Impact on Wildlife

Polar bears and marine life are the most affected by climate changes because they are heavily reliant on specific habitats. The U.S. Fish & Wildlife Service

agency has reported a decline in polar bear populations in Alaska and Canada as well as negative impacts on cool-water fish due to higher water temperatures.
